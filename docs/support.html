Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I save words to favorites?

A: Tap the heart icon on any word card to add it to your favorites. You can access your favorites from the main menu.

Q: Can I use the app offline?

A: Yes! All 10,000 words are stored locally on your device. You can study anytime, anywhere without an internet connection.

Q: How do I change the language?

A: Go to Settings and select your preferred language. We support 25 languages for translations.

Q: How do I remove ads?

A: You can purchase the "Remove Ads" option from the Settings menu to enjoy an ad-free experience.

Q: How do I restore my purchase?

A: Go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases" to restore your previous ad-free purchase.

Q: How does the pronunciation feature work?

A: Tap the speaker icon on any word card to hear the correct pronunciation using text-to-speech technology.
