1. Introduction

English Word 10000 ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our App, we and our third-party partners may automatically collect certain information:

Data Type Purpose Linked to Identity Used for Tracking
Device ID (IDFA) Third-party advertising, Analytics Yes Yes
Purchase History App functionality (Remove ads feature) Yes No

2.2 Information You Provide

The App does not require you to create an account or provide personal information such as name, email, or phone number.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

3.1 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data for personalized advertising purposes, including:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA)
  • IP address
  • Usage data and ad interaction

Learn more at Google's Privacy Policy.

3.2 Apple StoreKit (In-App Purchase)

We use Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchases. Purchase transactions are processed entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information. Learn more at Apple's Privacy Policy.

3.3 MyMemory Translation API

We use MyMemory API for word translations. Only the words you request to translate are sent to this service. No personal information is shared.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Third-party Advertising: To display relevant advertisements
  • Analytics: To understand app usage and improve our services
  • App Functionality: To enable features such as ad removal after purchase
  • Performance Improvement: To identify and fix bugs and crashes

5. Data Tracking

Our App uses tracking for advertising purposes. The following data may be used for tracking across apps and websites owned by other companies:

  • Device ID (IDFA)
  • Product Interaction Data
  • Advertising Data

You can limit ad tracking by adjusting your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings ??Privacy & Security ??Tracking ??Toggle off "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

6. Data Storage

Your vocabulary learning data (favorites, learned words, quiz results, and settings) is stored locally on your device. We do not store any of your personal data on external servers.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:
